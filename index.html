Every Frame a Color
A look at the use of colors in MCU movies

Every movie is represented by a color timeline, showing how colors change throughout each movie, and how colors of each movie differ from one another. See if you can identify a movie's key moments from the colors :)

Images are made by extracting the average colors of each frame in the movie, plotting the frame color as a thin color bar, then arranging them in chronoloigcal order.

Created this as my way of saying thank you for the 10 years of awesome. Ideas and code base for the images come from various Reddit posts. The name "Every Frame a Color" took inspirations from the YouTube channel and Podcast "Every Frame a Painting".
